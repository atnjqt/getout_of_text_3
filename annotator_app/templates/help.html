Workflow Overview

üìù Typical Workflow

1. Load Data: Upload or select KWIC concordance files in the Annotator

2. Annotate: Review each keyword instance and classify as literal, figurative, neither, or unclear

3. Analyze: Use linguistic analysis tools (Morphology, POS, N-grams, etc.) to explore patterns

4. Visualize: Generate frequency distributions and clustering visualizations

5. Export: Download annotations and analysis results via Concordance or Analysis pages

6. Experiment (Optional): Test AI prototypes for automated analysis and insights

üí° Research Note

The Annotator, AI Summary, and Embedding tools are exploratory prototypes investigating novel applications of large language models (LLMs) and neural network embeddings in corpus linguistics research. These features represent ongoing experiments in computational linguistics methodology and may be refined or expanded based on research findings.
